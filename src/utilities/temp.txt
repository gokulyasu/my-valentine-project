Un kooda irukka enakku romba pidikkum.
Unna love panradhu enakku proud.
Unna lose panna enakku bayam.
Life la evlo ups and downs vandhaalum, naan un side la dhaan iruppen. Sandhoshathukum, kashtathukum.
Thanks da Janani… en life ku vandhadhukku.
